Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll students into the system?

To enroll students, navigate to the enrollment section in the dashboard, fill out the student details form, and submit it. The system will then add the student to the database.

Can I update student information after enrollment?

Yes, you can update student information at any time. Navigate to the student profile, click on the edit button, make the necessary changes, and save them.

How do I generate student reports?

To generate student reports, go to the reports section, select the type of report you need (e.g., grades, attendance), choose the criteria (e.g., class, date range), and click on generate report.

Is there a way to communicate with students and parents through the system?

Yes, the system has a messaging feature. You can send messages to individual students, groups of students, or parents. Notifications will be sent via email or app notifications.

How do I handle student attendance?

To manage attendance, go to the attendance section, mark student attendance for each session, and save it. The system will keep a record of attendance for reporting purposes.
